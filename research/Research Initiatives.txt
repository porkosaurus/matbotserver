Creative Activities
Research and creative activities are an important part of CSUN's learning culture. Students work with faculty mentors on projects that address real-life challenges, preparing them for successful careers.
David Nazarian College of Business and Economics
Hosts an annual competition for entrepreneurial students to develop new products and ventures.
Learn more at https://www.csun.edu/entrepreneurship/8th-annual-jeff-marine-bull-ring-new-venture-competition
College of Engineering and Computer Science
Conducts research-based senior design projects in various engineering fields.
Learn more at https://www.csun.edu/~pbishay/projects.php
College of Health and Human Development
Produces dance performances that combine artistic expression with scientific research on physiology and performance.
College of Humanities
Organizes exhibits exploring cultural themes and community building through art.
Learn more at https://library.csun.edu/SCA/LibraryExhibits/low-and-slow
College of Science and Mathematics
Arranges field trips for alumni, faculty, and students to explore natural environments.
Learn more at https://news.csun.edu/college-of-science-and-mathematics/38th-fall-field-frolic-a-frolic-of-ice-and-fire-photos/
College of Social and Behavioral Sciences
Engages students in drone-based environmental research projects to benefit state parks.
Tseng College
Produces reports on workforce trends and educational needs in Southern California.
Learn more at https://tsengcollege.csun.edu/sites/default/files/aboutus/LAEDC-CSUN_Addressing_the_Knowledge_Worker_Talent_Gap_9-1-20-ADA.pdf
University Library
Faculty members contribute to books on university history and digital literacy.
